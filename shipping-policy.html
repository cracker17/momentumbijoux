Shipping Policy

Fast, tracked shipping on every order worldwide. Here's what to expect.

Last updated: June 2026

We ship fast, with tracking on every order worldwide. Here's exactly what to expect from the moment you check out.

Order Processing

Because our keepsakes are personalized and made to order, each item is produced specially for you. Orders are typically prepared and dispatched within 1–3 business days of purchase. During peak seasons and major holidays, processing may take slightly longer — we'll always keep you updated.

Shipping Costs

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your destination and the items in your cart, so you only ever pay accurate, transparent rates. Tracking is included on every shipment, and we work hard to dispatch your order as quickly as possible.

Estimated Delivery Times

Delivery estimates below are in addition to the processing time above and begin once your order has shipped:

  • United States & Canada: 5–9 business days
  • United Kingdom & Europe: 6–10 business days
  • Australia & New Zealand: 7–12 business days
  • Rest of the world: 7–15 business days

These are estimates, not guarantees. Delivery windows can be affected by carrier delays, customs processing, weather, and other factors outside our control.

Order Tracking

As soon as your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and link. You can also track your order anytime using the Track My Order link in our menu. Please allow up to 48 hours for tracking information to update after you receive it.

Customs, Duties & Taxes

For international orders, your package may be subject to import duties, taxes, or customs fees levied by the destination country. These charges are determined by your local authorities and are the responsibility of the recipient. Momentum Bijoux has no control over these fees and cannot predict their amount.

Incorrect or Incomplete Addresses

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We are not able to reroute a package once it has shipped, and we are not responsible for orders delivered to an address entered incorrectly. If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, we'll contact you to arrange re-delivery.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your tracking hasn't updated in a while or your order seems lost in transit, please reach out — we'll investigate with the carrier and make it right.

Questions about a shipment? Email support@momentumbijoux.com with your order number and we'll help right away.
